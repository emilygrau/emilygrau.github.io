About Me

Hey there! I'm Emily, a third-year student at Georgia Tech pursuing a BS of Computer Engineering. I had gone into my first year of college as a future Civil Engineer, but shorly after taking "Computing for Engineers" where I learned MATLAB, I fell in love with coding. At the end of the semester I officially made the switch to Computer Engineering (despite the echoing fears of my friends). You, like many others, may ask why CmpE and not CS? To that I say hardware has always intereseted me. Especially in an age where the future is technology, learning how it all works opens so many more opportunities for me.

Now, as a future Computer Engineer, I have experience in a few different high-level languages -- namely, MATLAB, C/C++, and HTML. I am also knowledgable in low-level MIPS Assembly. Everyday I am coding and doing my best to advance in the languages I already know and love, as well as learning new languages to add to my arsenal. I was not exposed to Computer Science before college and I am very lucky to go to a school that really pushes students to learn things outside their majors.
